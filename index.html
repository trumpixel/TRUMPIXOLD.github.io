Project Description

Trump Pixel World is a creative digital project that brings a pixelated version of Donald Trump's world to life. It begins with initializing the concept and designing a pixel art prototype, followed by building an interactive website (trumpixold.netlify.app) to showcase the project and engage users. The third phase involves creating a metaverse experience, featuring a virtual, pixelated White House with interactive elements and immersive features. Finally, the project focuses on fostering a vibrant community through social platforms, events, and regular updates, ensuring a collaborative and engaging environment for fans and users.
